How To Check For Threat Prevention Auto Deploy Credentials

To determine whether or not Auto Deploy is enabled, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Threat Prevention Console
  2. In the Policy Center on the left side of the console, click on Agents.
  3. Along the top bar of the Agents screen click on Configure Auto Deploy.
  4. In the Configure Auto Deploy popup window you can check for the following:
    1. Auto Deploy is enabled if the Enable Auto Deploy checkbox is checked and the form is usable.
    2. Credentials are stored if the Login and Password credential options are filled out.
