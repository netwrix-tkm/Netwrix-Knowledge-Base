How to Re-Register a Deleted Device on Change Tracker

In order to re-register a device that has previously been deleted from Change Tracker, please follow the step-by-step guide below!

Step-by-step guide

  1. Log into the Change Tracker Console.
  2. Head into the Settings tab.
  3. Navigate into theAgents & Devicestab.
  4. Change the filter on the top left corner to[No Group Filter]

5. A list of all the deleted devices should now be visible within theAgents & Devicespage and there should be an option toRe-Register the device as seen in the example below:

6. Simply click on theRe-Register button and your device will be back.

The steps above should re-register your device successfully, and you should now be able to see the device online on the Change Tracker Console.
