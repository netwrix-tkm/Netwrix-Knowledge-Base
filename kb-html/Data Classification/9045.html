Required Term Clue Not Working

Symptom

The term containing a Required Term clue is not being applied, while the Required Term clue is applied with all other conditions for the term met.

Cause

The Required Term clue only meets classification due to a term boost and would not be classified otherwise. This is a known issue affecting the v5.7.1.23.

Resolution

Replace a Required Term clue with a mandatory Term Boost clue. The term being boosted should be represented by the required term.
