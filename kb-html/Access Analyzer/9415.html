Reports in custom group do not display in reporting console.

Symptom

When creating a report in a custom group. After publishing the report, it does not show up in the reporting web interface.

The customer group also shows up with a truncated name.

 

Cause

Access Analyzer truncates "Jobs" or "_Jobs" from the end of all group names as this is an internal name. 

The truncated name then gets displayed in the Reporting console and settings for published reports in this group may be have unpredictably.

Example: A group named "Custom_Jobs" would display in the reporting module as "Custom_" and any reports under this group may not apply settings appropriately.

Resolution

To correct the issue, rename any custom group using the word "Jobs", to something that does not include the word "Jobs" to prevent truncation.
