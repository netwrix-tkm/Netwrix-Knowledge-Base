How to Retrieve a List of Expiring SSL/TLS Certificates and Their Expiration Dates

How can I obtain a list of SSL/TLS certificates and their expiration dates?

Open an Admin PowerShell or ISE window. 

NOTE Expiration dates are noted as NotAfter for when they certificate will no longer be valid.

Modified the following as needed.

## To filter the output of the following between the Sort and Format pipe the data through |Where-Object  Subject -like "*EDIT-AS-NEEDED*"

Get-ChildItem -Path Cert:\LocalMachine\ -Recurse |Select-Object -Property * |Sort-Object NotAfter -Descending |

Format-Table Thumbprint,FriendlyName, NotAfter, PSParentPath -AutoSize
