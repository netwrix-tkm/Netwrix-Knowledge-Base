[UPDATE]- How to export the list of computers, users or devices

To export the entire list of computers, users, or devices, the steps are similar, but the menu is different.

For computers please open the console and go to Device Control -> Computers, for users go to Device Control -> Users, and for devices go to Device Control -> Devices

At the bottom of the page please click on Choose action and select either Export list of Computers or Schedule export list if you prefer to have a scheduled export.

For the manual export, after you click on Export list of computers, you will see a banner at the top of the page mentioning where the export is found.

For the scheduled export, after you click on Schedule export list, you can select the frequency of the export and the start date. Once you press schedule you will see a banner at the top of the page stating that the export has been scheduled.

For both methods, the list can be found in System Maintenance -> Exported Entities on the List of exports page.

By pressing the Actions menu you can download or delete the list.
