    <h2>Question:</h2>
    <p>Why is my printer detected by Endpoint Protector as a USB Storage Device?</p>

    <h2>Resolution:</h2>
    <p>Some printers include built-in storage, which may be the case for your device. The printing function is handled by the "Printers" device type, while the storage component is managed by the "USB Storage Device" device type.</p>
