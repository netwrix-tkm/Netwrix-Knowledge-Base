Locating Archived Articles

Overview

This article provides instructions on how to discover archived articles. The Netwrix Knowledge Center hosts knowledge base and documentation articles. Documentation articles are updated and archived depending on the lifecycle of the product. KB articles vary in their lifecycle depending on the covered topic, status of the documentation development, etc. In some cases, KB articles can be archived with traces left in products or other live articles.

Instructions

Refer to the following steps to locate archived articles:

  1. Copy the article ID from the KB link. Refer to the following example:

    https://helpcenter.netwrix.com/bundle/z-kb-articles-salesforce/page/%article_ID%.html

  2. Paste the article ID to the following link and proceed to the new link:

    https://nwxcorp.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/General__kav/%article_ID%/view
