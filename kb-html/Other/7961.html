Issue: Summary:
Sensitive Data and Permissions jobs blocking other SPIDs
 
Behavior Before Fix:
Customer is reporting issue where SA processes are locking tables and preventing other queries from executing during the day.
 
Behavior After Fix:
The SA SQL queries now have unlock hints that should allow it to ignore other SQL processes and so that other SQL processes can ignore it.
