Issue: Summary:
Sat-4192 PowerShell DC: Improve security for remote execution
Currently we are being pen-tested at a customer where they found the ability to retrieve a domain administrator password, they referenced the PowerShellDC. While we are still waiting for the details to come through, there are some simple fixes we can instrument in order to mitigate some risks.
Credentials are no longer put into the log after hotfix is applied.
