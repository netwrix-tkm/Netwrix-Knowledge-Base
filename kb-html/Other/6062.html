Netapp Bug - Increased FPolicy latency under a low or sporadic workload with netapp 9.8

Summary: Netapp Bug - Increased FPolicy latency under a low or sporadic workload with netapp 9.8

 

Issue:


We told customers that we support netapp 9.8.
?
There's a caveat that we've just become aware of. 
?
Kevin and I were on a call with Houston Methodists and NetApp Support. 
?
The customer observed increased latency after upgrading to 9.8. 
?
The implemented all the tips suggested by NetApp: increase size of the send buffer, add a data LIFs for each cluster node. Nothing helped. NetApp said that the problem was in SAM. 
?

I asked the customer to set up another call with NetApp and Stealthbits. Which we had today. 
?
And only today they confirmed that it is a NetApp bug. It was recently identified in 9.8 and they came with a workaround just yesterday. 
?
Here's the bug:
https://mysupport.netapp.com/site/bugs-online/product/ONTAP/BURT/1415152
 
?
In a nutshell, if the load on SVM is less then 1500 events/second, your latency is very likely to increase. 
?
Netapp considers the bug high priority. There's no ETA yet, but it should be fixed soon. 
 
The workaround provided in the ticket requires a reboot of each node of the cluster. 
?
The support engineer claimed that the parameter will be removed automatically as soon as the bug is fixed. 

 

Instructions:  If customers are wanting to upgrade their netapp to 9.8 we should suggest them to wait until this bug is fixed.  If customers already have 9.8 there is a workaround suggested if they currently have latency issues.
