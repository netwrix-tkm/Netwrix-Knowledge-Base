Hotfix: AIC_11.0_003

Summary:

SMPC-9920 \ Escalations 54828: AIC not showing all groups

  • Issue/Previous behavior: When setting resources' access groups, the available ones were the groups directly ACLd to the resource. Customer wants the ability to pick either the top level or the nested groups.
  • Fix/Enhancement: Now the list of available access groups comes from the Expanded Permissions view, which includes nested groups.
