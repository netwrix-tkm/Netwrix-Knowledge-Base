Summary: Textsearch DC removes spaces in loaded data
Issue/Request: When the audited document contained multiple spaces between text, they were reduced to a single space in the job's results. This resulted in issues for the client when matching data.
Fix/Enhancement: Now multiple spaces are being respected and reflected in the job's results.
