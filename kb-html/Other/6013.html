Summary:

I-7501 SUVP 21-11 | Kerberos, DCsync or LDAP events may be lost or incomplete after applying MSFT updates, release: 11/09/2021

Behavior before Fix:

Events may be lost or incolplete for the following SI policies:

  • 2012 Server:
    • SI Authentication Monitoring, Lockdown and Analytics polices for Kerberos events.
    • LDAP Monitoring and Lockdown.
    • AD Replication Monitoring and Lockdown.
  • 2012R2, 2016 and 2019 Servers:
    • SI Authentication Monitoring, Lockdown and Analytics polices for Kerberos events.

The SI policies listed above may not work as expected as a result of missed or incomplete events.

Behavior after Fix:

Signatures updated; SI policies must be fully functional.
