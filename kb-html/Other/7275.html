Netwrix Website Login Information

Summary: Information about how website logins work.

Issue:  
Once someone signs up for our website, if they're a customer, partner or employee, we approve them so they get access to different areas of the website, such as

  • documentation
  • partner portal
  • training materials

This is a manual process and usually takes about a day.
