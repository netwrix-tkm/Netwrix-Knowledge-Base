Result: Administrator restricted access to users of your domain.

I get the "Result: Administrator restricted access to users of your domain" on the Self-Service portal during enroll or password reset procedure.
To resolve the issue make sure that the domain of the user(s) account that receives the error appears in the list of managed domains
  1. Go to the Administrative portal (by default http://<prm server>/pm/admin)
  2. Click the Domains button
  3. Make sure that the domain of the user(s) account that receives the error appears in the list of managed domains.
  4. Make sure there are no additinal symbols in the domain name (spaces, unnesessary characters)
  5. Add the domain if necessary
