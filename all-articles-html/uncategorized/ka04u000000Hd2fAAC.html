Issue: Summary:

  1. Add comment field on SAM console "agent" screen
  2. Exchange | Add support for Exchange 2016 CU16 and 2019 CU5 (release 03/17/20)
  3. 2016 & 2019 Servers | No NTLM events; Kerberos events may be lost or incomplete after applying MS Updates, released 03/17/20

Behavior After Fix:
Comment field has been added to agents; Exchange, NTLM, and Kerberos monitoring has been updated to support latest windows updates.
