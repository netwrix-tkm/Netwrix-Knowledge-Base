Hotfix: SIWindowsAgent_7.0.1.609_SI-4096

Summary:

SI-4096 50869 - UAC not alerting on 1-bit changes when configured explicitly for that change.

Behavior before Fix:

The following logic was used for evaluating uac filter:

for 'set' case: (oldUAC^newUAC) &newUAC) ^ config.SetBitMask

for 'clear' case: (oldUAC ^ newUAC) & oldUAC) ^ config.ClearBitMask

Behavior after Fix:

new logic used for evaluating uac filter:

for 'set' case: (oldUAC^newUAC) &newUAC) & config.SetBitMask

for 'clear' case: (oldUAC ^ newUAC) & oldUAC) & config.ClearBitMask
