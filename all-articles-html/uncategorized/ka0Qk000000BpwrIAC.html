Question

How to delete domains in PingCastle?

Answer

In order to delete a domain on PingCastle please follow the next steps:

  1. Check the ID of the domain, it is visible in the URL once you access the domain from the web version.

  2. To proceed with deleting the domain, please navigate to the following URL: http://localhost/Database/DeleteDomain/XX and click Proceed.
