How To Create Orphan Group Update Schedule

Applies To:

GroupID 11

Business Requirement:

We have multiple groups in GroupID who used to have a primary owner assigned to them as well as additional owner(s). However, their primary owner field was emptied out because either someone removed it manually or primary owner�s AD accounts got deleted from AD after they left. Can we auto-assign a primary owner to such groups?

More Information:

Such groups are called orphan groups in GroupID. We can create and schedule an �Orphan Group Update� job, which is responsible for assigning a primary owner to groups that do not have one. For this, the orphan group must have at least one additional owner, since the job promotes a group�s additional owner as its primary owner.

When an Orphan Group Update job is run, the first additional owner in the additional owners� list (be it a user, contact, or security group) is promoted as the primary owner, and a notification is sent to the promoted owner. In case of a security group being promoted as the primary owner, a notification is sent to all members of the group.
