Whitelist/exclude EPP in Crowdstrike

Question:

How do I whitelist/exclude EPP in Crowdstrike?

Answer:

In order to whitelist/exclude EPP in Crowdstrike please add the EPP application process names below, based on the OS type:

�

For Windows:

- path: C:\Program Files\CoSoSys\Endpoint Protector\EPPService.exe

- application: EPPService.exe

�

For Mac:

- path: /Applications/EndpointProtectorClient.app/Contents/MacOS/EppClient

- application: EndpointProtectorClient.app
