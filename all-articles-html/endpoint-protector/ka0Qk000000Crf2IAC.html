[UPDATE]- How to add new entry in Justification List

To add a new entry to the Justification List, first open your EPP console and navigate to the bottom of it, to the System Parameters menu, and then to the�User Remediation section.

Here, we will create a Justification list that will appear to the end user when they need an explanation to remediate the file.
- Click on the�ADD button.�
- On the Justification tab, you can add a question, such as why the Print screen is required.
- Then, change the status to enable and the reason to yes.
- The last step is to save it.

Once the above steps are finished:
Please navigate to the top side of the console, select the Content Aware Protection menu, and create a policy to apply the User remediation.
Here, you need to select the OS, the name of the policy, and at the policy action button, select from the drop-down Block and Remediate.
On the Policy Exit points, click the checkmark for�Print Screen.
The next step will be to scroll down to the Policy entities and select the department/groups/computers/users where you want to apply the policy, then click on save from the bottom of the page.
Next, you will need to go to one of the endpoints where the policy is applied, open the EPP client, and update the Policies.
To test if the policy is working, please try to use the print screen function.
