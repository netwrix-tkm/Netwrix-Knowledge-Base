[UPDATE]- Will Content Aware Protection block sensitive content files from the "Allowed File" Allowlist?

Allowed Files Allowlists are custom groups of files you exclude from Endpoint Protector sensitive content detection, available for both Content Aware Protection and eDiscovery modules

The Allowed Files can be found under Denylists and Allowlists -> Allowlists-> Allowed File:
