[UPDATE]- Implementing User Remediation

Although it is recommended to turn on User Remediation after configuring a Blocking Policy for Content Aware Protection, we will cover this item here.

To turn on User Remediation, navigate to the Content Aware Protection Policy(s) where restriction is intended and choose to edit the "Policy Action:" field. By selecting "Block and Remediate",the User Remediation feature will be enabled the next time the endpoint connects to the Endpoint Protector Server:
