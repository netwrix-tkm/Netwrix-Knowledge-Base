[UPDATE]- How to add specific devices to allow list!

To add specific devices to the allow list, you need to open the EPP web console, then navigate to the Device Control menu and select the Global Rights sub-menu.
Once the page is loaded, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the ADD button.

After the button is pressed, a new window will appear allowing you to add the specific device to the Allowlist.

Fill in the requested details regarding your device and click next, and you will be required to add the device, in our case, as a new device.
