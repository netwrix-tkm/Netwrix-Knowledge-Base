[UPDATE]- How to prevent black screen when accessing a machine remotely.

This issue might occur if a CAP policy has "Print screen" blocking enabled and also the "Advanced Printer and MTP Scanning" setting checked.

The "Print screen" blocking is preventing to capture the content of the windows on the screen when it is accessed remotely or even is sharing the screen on a meeting.

To make it work, "Print screen" blocking in the CAP policy has to be disabled.
