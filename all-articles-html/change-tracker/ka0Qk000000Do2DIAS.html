Please Note:


In some cases users may notice that the NNTInfo driver is running, however they are experience intermittent interruption of who made the change information.

This mainly occurs on extremely busy and noisy systems with a large volume of changes. The kernel buffer which is used to capture the who made the change information may be overflowing, so our agent may be unable to capture the who made the change information in this case. Keep in mind that because our agent is a kernel level agent, although excluding the events in Change Tracker will stop the reporting of the events, these events are still being generated at the kernel level and so, the agent is seeing them, but choosing not to report them to the hub server based on the predefined exclusion.

Large volumes of changes will impact the agents ability to capture who made the change information depending on the volume.
