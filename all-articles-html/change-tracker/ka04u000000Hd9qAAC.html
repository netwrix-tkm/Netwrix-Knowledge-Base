How to delete a device in Log Tracker

Occasionally it is necessary to delete a device in Log Tracker. Sometimes it might be a device that was added as a result of a test message or it could be a device whose IP address has changed due to network changes.

To delete a device, go to the menu option Messages > Catalogs+ > Devices and then select the "Advanced >" button near the top of the screen. At the bottom of the screen, click "Edit >" next to "Delete Devices By List". Here you will have the option of inputting a device IP address manually or selecting multiple addresses from a list. To manually input devices, simply input the IP addresses in the box. To select multiple devices, click the "Select >" button to select them from a list. In either case the IP addresses of the device will be placed in the box. Click "Delete >" to delete the device or "< Cancel" to cancel your changes and return to the previous menu.
