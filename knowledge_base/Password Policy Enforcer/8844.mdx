## Symptom

After the recent upgrade, Password Policy Enforcer (PPE) users do not see any password policy requirements.

## Causes

One or more of the following causes may affect your PPE installation. Refer to the list of possible causes:

The PPE upgrade is not completed.

The PPE integration with third-party credential providers is misconfigured (e.g., Duo Security, Global Protect, etc.).

Firewall rules for PPE are missing or misconfigured.

In PPE v10.2 and newer, there's a protocol mismatch in PPE Client and Server.
